FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of collectibles are your cases suited for?
Our cases are used for knives, swords, coins, arrowheads, medals, military memorabilia, jewelry, firearms accessories, and much more. If you're unsure which case fits your items, call us and we'll help.
Can I order a case in a non-standard size?
Yes. We offer made-to-order, non-stock cases at an additional charge. Contact us with your exact dimensions and we'll confirm feasibility and timeline.
What wood species are available?
Cherry, walnut, and oak — each with a hand-rubbed satin finish. Optional hardwood feet available in matching species.
Can I change the velvet inlay color?
Replacement velvet inserts are available in black, blue, red, and green for all major case series. Custom colors can be arranged at order time.
How are orders shipped?
Every case is individually boxed with protective packaging. We use tempered safety glass in aluminum cases and pack each order carefully.
Do you sell Winchester Trademark and John Primble knives?
Yes — through our Blue Grass Cutlery division. Contact us directly for current inventory and pricing.
How do I place an order?
Call (937) 549-3902 or email sales@s-denterprises.com. No automated systems — just real people who know their products.
What is your return policy?
We stand behind everything we ship. If a product arrives damaged or defective, contact us right away and we’ll make it right.
