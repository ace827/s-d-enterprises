Lucite Displays & Accessories

Clear acrylic stands, easels, glass domes, and specialty holders for showcasing individual pieces.

About Lucite Displays

Versatile Acrylic for Any Setting

Our clear acrylic lucite stands are perfect for store windows, home displays, and office settings. Bring collectibles that have been sitting at the back of a collection back into the spotlight.

  • Easels in multiple sizes — 1¾" to 12" high
  • Double-bend easels for heavier pieces
  • Label easels for signage and identification
  • Sword and hat/helmet stands
  • Rifle and pistol stands
  • Glass domes with wood bases (4½" to 12" high)
  • Lucite box cases with wood bases
  • Cube risers from 2" to 6" high
