Aluminum Display Cases

Lightweight, portable, and secure — anodized aluminum cases built for collectors on the go.

Features

Durable Aluminum, Built to Travel

Allstate Manufacturing Co. — our aluminum division — produces anodized cases that go wherever your collection goes. Show-ready and secure with tempered glass and keyed locks.

  • Full-length continuous hinge for added strength
  • Solid Masonite bottoms
  • Built-in support arms
  • Tamper-proof keyed cylinder lock
  • All-riveted construction
  • Tempered safety glass
  • Deluxe color bottom inserts available
  • Individually boxed for safe shipping
