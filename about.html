About S & D & T Enterprises

Over 50 years of American craftsmanship from Manchester, Ohio.

Our Story

A Family Business Built on Quality

S & D & T Enterprises, INC. was founded in Manchester, Ohio in the early 1970s as a small family operation. Over the decades the business grew into a multi-division enterprise serving collectors, dealers, and enthusiasts across the country.

Today we carry more than 32 years of industry experience and operate three specialized divisions — each focused on a distinct corner of the display case market. Our commitment has never changed: build the best product at a fair price, backed by personal service.

Every product is handcrafted in the USA using only the finest materials. We individually box each order for protective shipment and stand behind every case we ship.

Our Three Divisions

Specialized Expertise, Under One Roof

Each division brings a focused skill set to serve a distinct segment of the collector market.

🏭

Allstate Manufacturing Co.

Our aluminum division — specializing in anodized aluminum display cases. Lightweight, secure, and built for collectors who need portability without sacrificing quality.

📦

Ruddles Mills Products

Specializing in riker-type black chipboard displays. Trusted by coin collectors, militaria dealers, and arrowhead enthusiasts for generations.

🗡

Blue Grass Cutlery

Specializing in authentic Winchester Trademark knives and John Primble knives — collector-grade American cutlery paired with display cases built to show them at their best.
