Wooden Display Cases

Handcrafted from solid cherry, walnut, and oak with brass hardware and rich velveteen inlay.

Craftsmanship

Built from Solid Hardwood

Every wooden display case is handcrafted from genuine solid wood — no veneers, no shortcuts. Finished with a grain-enhancing satin finish that highlights the natural beauty of each piece.

  • Available in cherry, walnut, and oak
  • Solid brass hinges & brass-plated clasp locks
  • Easy glass replacement
  • Scratch-preventing felt pads on case bottoms
  • Rich red foam velveteen inlay (blue, black, green available)
  • Strengthening biscuit & spline corners on 2"+ cases
  • Grain-enhancing, hand-rubbed satin finish
  • Optional hardwood feet for stand-up display
  • Custom non-stock sizes available
