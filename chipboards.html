About Riker Cases

The Collector's Display Standard

Our Ruddles Mills Products division produces riker-type chipboards trusted by coin dealers, militaria collectors, and arrowhead enthusiasts for generations.

  • Glass-topped lid, easily removable
  • Extra heavy, plated, non-rusting pins
  • Pin inserts on display's longest side
  • Black velvet backing on chipboard
  • Polyester fill for cushioning and display
  • Wide range of sizes available
