Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions, Honest Answers

Still not sure? Here are answers to the questions we hear most often.

What types of collectibles are your display cases suited for?
Our cases are used for an enormous variety of collectibles — knives, swords, coins, arrowheads, medals, military memorabilia, jewelry, arrowheads, firearms accessories, and more. If you're not sure which case fits your items, call us and we'll help you find the right fit.
Can I order a case in a size that isn't listed?
Yes. We offer made-to-order, non-stock cases for an additional charge. Contact us with your exact dimensions and we'll let you know what's possible and the timeline involved. This applies to both our wooden cases and custom insert configurations.
What wood species are your cases available in?
Our wooden display cases come in three solid hardwoods: cherry, walnut, and oak. Each is finished with a grain-enhancing, hand-rubbed satin finish that highlights the natural beauty of the wood. Optional hardwood feet are available in matching species.
Can I replace or change the velvet inlay color?
Absolutely. Replacement color velvet inserts with foam backing are available separately for all major case series. Colors include black, blue, red, and green. If you want a non-standard color at time of order, let us know and we'll arrange it for an additional charge.
How are the cases shipped, and is the glass protected?
Every case is individually boxed with protective packaging specifically designed for our products. We use tempered safety glass in our aluminum cases and take great care to ensure everything arrives intact. If you receive a damaged item, contact us immediately and we'll make it right.
Do you sell the Winchester Trademark knives and John Primble knives individually?
Yes — through our Blue Grass Cutlery division, we carry authentic Winchester Trademark knives and John Primble knives. These are collector-grade American cutlery pieces. Contact us directly for current inventory and pricing on available models.
How do I place an order?
The easiest way is to call us at (937) 549-3902 or email us at sales@s-denterprises.com. We'll discuss what you need, confirm availability and pricing, and walk you through the order process directly. There's no automated system here — just real people who know their products.
What is your return policy?
We stand behind the quality of our products. If there is a defect or damage from shipping, contact us right away and we will work with you to resolve the issue. Please review our full return policy on our website or ask us directly when placing your order.
